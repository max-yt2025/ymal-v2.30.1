COOKIE POLICY
========================================================================

OUR PHILOSOPHY: YMAL uses minimal cookies and absolutely zero cross-site tracking cookies. We respect your digital privacy.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?
Cookies are small configuration text files saved to your local storage device when visiting websites to store functional display parameters.

2. ESSENTIAL & FUNCTIONAL COOKIES WE USE:
* session_id: Maintains your active window session for the duration of your visit. (Type: Essential / Duration: Temporary Session)
* preferences: Remembers your selected dark/light themes and language settings. (Type: Functional / Duration: 30 Days)
* consent: Remembers your cookie acknowledgement choices. (Type: Essential / Duration: 1 Year)

3. WHAT WE DO NOT USE:
* NO Tracking Cookies: We never log your long-term browsing behaviors.
* NO Target Ad Cookies: We do not serve personalized, profile-tracking advertisements.
* NO Third-Party Cookies: We do not permit third-party data tracking scripts on our domains.

4. MANAGING COOKIES
You may freely block or clear cookies using YMAL Browser's built-in clearing controls or settings panels. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the persistence of your local preference setups.
